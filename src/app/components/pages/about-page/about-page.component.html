About The Artist

Karolina Serrano, known under the pseudonym "Santalma" is a versatile artist born in Colombia, who from an early age manifested great sensitivity and creative concern, which led her to explore art from different perspectives. She was born surrounded by art and raised in a family of recognized painters and sculptors.

Santalma's artwork is inspired by the colors, flavors, and rhythms of her native land. Its biodiversity, the joy of its people, the warm water beaches, its celebrations, and Carnivals.

Guided by de desire and Inspired by the idea of carrying a constant celebration that cheers the soul and sparks joy to those who wear her creations, Santalma hand-paints one-of-a-kind accessories resulting in unique and joyfully vibrant pieces.
